The Problem

People all over the world are on social media and they love sharing their opinions! Political discussions are especially huge online but many people don't search out for different opinions. People end up sharing the same stuff to the same people over and over again, perpetuating the lie that their opinion is the only one based in fact!

What do we do?

side.chat aims to end these online "echo chambers‚Äù by allowing you to identify bias in news as part of a dynamic online social and mobile environment. We have jettisoned the traditional upvote/downvote system in favour of a political left/right rating system. side.chat allows you to determine and rate whether discussions surrounding posted articles are politically biased. You can also contribute by posting your own articles and comments. Join the side.chat community and can see for yourself whether the news you are consuming is healthily balanced.
